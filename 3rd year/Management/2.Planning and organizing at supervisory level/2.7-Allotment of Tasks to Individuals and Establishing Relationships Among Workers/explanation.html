2.7 Allotment of Tasks to Individuals and Establishing Relationships Among Workers

Once the resources are organized and the right people are selected for the right jobs, the next step is to properly divide and assign tasks among employees.

Steps in Task Allotment:

  • Break down work into different tasks.
  • Assign tasks to workers based on their skills.
  • Establish a chain of command (who reports to whom).
  • Promote teamwork by ensuring employees work together efficiently.
  • Monitor and adjust if tasks need to be redistributed.

Why is establishing relationships among workers important?

  • It prevents confusion about roles and responsibilities.
  • Encourages teamwork and cooperation.
  • Helps in faster decision-making.
  • Reduces workplace conflicts by defining clear reporting structures.
