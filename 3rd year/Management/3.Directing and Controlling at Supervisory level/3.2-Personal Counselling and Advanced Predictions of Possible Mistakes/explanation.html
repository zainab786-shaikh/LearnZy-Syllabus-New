3.2 Personal Counselling and Advanced Predictions of Possible Mistakes

What is Personal Counselling?

Supervisors often guide employees not only on work-related issues but also on personal matters that affect their performance. This is called personal counselling. It helps employees stay focused, motivated, and stress-free.

Predicting Mistakes in Advance

A good supervisor can foresee possible mistakes and take steps to prevent them.

Example:

Personal Counselling:

If an employee is frequently absent due to personal problems, the supervisor can talk to them, understand their issues, and offer flexible work options.

Predicting Mistakes:

In a bakery, if workers are rushing, they might mix ingredients incorrectly. A supervisor can prevent this by reminding them to double-check measurements.
